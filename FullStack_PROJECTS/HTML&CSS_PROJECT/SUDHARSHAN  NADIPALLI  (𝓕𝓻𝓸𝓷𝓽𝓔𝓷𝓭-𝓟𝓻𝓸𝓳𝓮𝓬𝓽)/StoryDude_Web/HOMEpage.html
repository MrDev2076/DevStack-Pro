About:

The Author

Radha

Radha is a homemaker. Her knowledge in Telugu literature and passion for writing has influenced her to start this blog with the help of her son. She uses to tell a story at the night to her son daily. Now her idea is to share all the stories in this blog for children. This blog helps All the parents to learn new stories and famous stories so that they can deliver to kids. We believe Storytelling the best way of teaching children, in this present generation the possibility of telling a story to children has decreased.

Our main motto is to reach more kids and deliver good molar stories. Building a good moral in children helps to build a good society.
